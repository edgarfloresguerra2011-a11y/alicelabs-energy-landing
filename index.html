The Market Advantage

Why Auction Beats Shopping Around

Utility Default = Overpaying

Businesses on default utility supply rates pay 25–40% more than the best available market rate — often without knowing a competitive alternative exists.

Competitive Bidding Forces Better Margins

When suppliers know they're competing in real-time against every other qualified provider in your market, they submit their sharpest pricing. That pressure benefits you directly.

Timing Matters — We Know When to Lock In

The best window for 2026 contracts is during shoulder seasons (spring and early fall). Bid On Energy's market indicators tell you exactly when spot prices and futures align for maximum savings.
